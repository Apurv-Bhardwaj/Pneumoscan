Our Story

Meet the passionate students behind Pneumoscan, driven by innovation and a vision to transform healthcare through technology

Apurv Bhardwaj

Co-Founder & Lead Developer

As a 20-year-old student at SRM University, Apurv has always been fascinated by the intersection of technology and healthcare. His journey in software development began with simple web applications, but his passion for AI and machine learning led him to explore more complex projects. At SRM, he's been actively involved in various technical clubs and hackathons, where he developed his expertise in deep learning and computer vision.

His vision for Pneumoscan stems from a personal experience with healthcare accessibility challenges in his community. This motivated him to create a solution that could make medical diagnosis more accessible and efficient.

Divyanshi Kochar

Co-Founder & AI Specialist

At 21, Divyanshi brings a unique perspective to Pneumoscan with her deep understanding of artificial intelligence and its applications in healthcare. Her academic journey at SRM University has been marked by excellence in computer science and a particular interest in medical AI applications.

She has been instrumental in developing the core AI algorithms that power Pneumoscan's analysis capabilities. Her research work focuses on improving the accuracy and efficiency of medical image analysis, making healthcare more accessible to underserved communities.
